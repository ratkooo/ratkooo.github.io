Who am I?

My name is Radovan Vyšňan, I was born in Poprad, Slovakia where I have lived my whole life. I have always taken pride in myself and I'm happy to come from said country. Ever since I can remember, I was always fiddling with things and trying to improve / make things work. I started school when I was 6 and finished my education in Slovakia by attending the Secondary School of Technology and Design in Poprad. I passed my final exams with almost perfect grades, so it motivated me to try even harder at this University. I studied a 4 year course, my degree was mechatronics. The thing I'm most grateful for that school gave me is the basics of programming and the ability to work with electrical circuits and components. I hope my horizons will broaden during the time spent here on HZ University, so I can lay a good foundation for my future career.

In my free time I usually play video games or spend times with my friends. Back in Slovakia I also used to skate quite a bit, but I still haven't really got back into it ever since I moved here. My favourite games ever are Skyrim and Dark Souls franchise.
