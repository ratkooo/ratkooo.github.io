Study Choice

I chose studying ICT because I always enjoyed working with computers and programming seems like the best way to earn a good salary and to have a career built upon it. My first idea was to go study in Denmark, but they made it much more difficult for international students to actually apply and get accepted for a school, so my second best option was Netherlands, and I got this school recommended by a student agency Information Planet.

I will probably want to expand my education by doing a Masters degree regarding ICT, I don't yet know what I would like to study, but I'm thinking about Game Design.

I studied mechatronics at my secondary school, and I found out that machinery and automatization stuff doesn't really interest me and I don't want to do anything regarding that in my life.
